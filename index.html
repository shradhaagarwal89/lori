How it Works?




How to list?


Step 1
List the product that you wish to lend by keying in the required information.

Step 2
LORI shall review the same to ensure that it qualifies with all the compliances and then take the product live.

Step 3
Your listing is now publicly visible. Cheers!




How to lend ?


Step 1: Confirmation
Renter pays the rent to LORI as confirmation.

Step 2: Lend
On the day, lend the item to the renter after confirming that it was lent in promised condition.

Step 3: Earn
Upon completion of rental cycle, provide feedback and request to ‘collect’ your earned amount, and the same shall be credited to you.







How to Rent (Borrow)?


Step 1
Type what you are looking for or search for it via applying various filters

Step 2
Sent in a request to rent the item by agreeing on the ‘hire fee’ & terms & conditions,and then await approval for the same from the lender

Step 3
Upon receiving confirmation from lender, pay the ‘hire fee + rental fee’ to confirm rental of the item

Step 4
You can either collect and return the item yourself from the lender as we will connect the two parties after collecting ‘hire fee’ or you can request for a pick up & drop of the rented item through our delivery partners for an extra delivery fee.







How to request for an unlisted item?


Did not find what you are looking for ? Don’t worry, just leave a message for us and we will send out a request for you.

Step 1
Search for what you are looking for by applying the correct filters.
Step 2
Simply tell us about the item and the area in which you are looking for it.
Step 3
As soon as someone around you has listed the item that you requested for, we shall notify you.

LORI shall look forward for you to rent it. We are always here to help.
